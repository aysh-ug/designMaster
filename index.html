Written on: 11/ May/ 2020 By Alfred

This article is mainly for those considering to buy a windows PC because there are so many brands to choose from. Which one should you trust?

My opinion is this: HP is the strongest brand if you want a windows PC. So am gona talk about HP here.

Key Features of a good HP laptop

  • RAM:
    Depending in the work you intend to do, a minimum of 4GB is fair enough.
  • HDD vs SSD STORAGE
    Well, SSD is a much faster and more expensive hard drive. It is the newer technology of the 2. However, most laptops have HDD except the newest. A good HDD size depends on the amount of data you intend to store. A minimum of 500 GB is sufficient. However it is very important to keep deleting old irrelevant data to create space on your pc regularly.
  • GRAPHICS CARD SIZE:
    This is an issue mainly for gamers, animators, architects and engineers who do heavy machine renders of artwork and code. NVIDIA provides the strongest graphics cards and the PCs that use NVIDIA are much stronger than the others.

    Am sure you have heard of duo core, i3, i5 and i7. Well, the higher the number here, the faster and stronger the PC shall be in performance, ofcourse more expensive too. So hey, ask which one it is. For basic work i3 is good enough. However for the architects, animators and engineers, their work might require greater performance so i suggest a minimum of i5.
